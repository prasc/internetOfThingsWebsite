What is IoT?

Internet of things (IOTâ€™s) explains the concept of integral network of devices to one another through the means of the Internet with a controlled mechanism to turn on and off (1). This allows a form of communication between these interconnected devices through the Internet which can help them to stay in sync to one another for example to complete a daily task such as making coffee in time (2).

Why is it useful for these devices to be connected?

The connection of these devices and allowing them to share data to one another facilitates user in collecting data, analysing the data, and producing an encounter or action contributing to learning and/or assisting in a task to be completed (2).
