Internet of Things

Applications of IoT

IOT application can be almost applied to any industry especially in this day and time.

Wearables:

This includes fit bits and smartwatches which monitor your physical activities such as steps, heart rate (5). These types of devices are considered to be small and energy efficient devices (6). In order to monitor, the devices consist of sensors to display these readings to the user (6).

Healthcare:

The healthcare industry is a huge industry consisting of doctors, nurses, patients etc. which all require important information to keep track of. A great example is monitoring their patients. In order to do this in an efficient manner, IOT improved patient care by allowing doctors to obtain real time data on the patient’s condition (6). IOT has also helped with the development of smart beds which also consists of sensors to monitor the patient’s vital signs (6).

Traffic Monitor

IOT in traffic monitor is very helpful to provide drivers real time data on traffic flow. This makes it very convenient for drivers to plan accordingly as the data would provide time of arrival, distance, accident information etc (6). This data is provided by mobile phones which consist of sensors to gather and share the information through applications such as Google maps or Waze (5). IOT is also helpful to provide information about “smart cities” to understand the global problems faced in different countries (5). Information such as clean water, air quality, population density etc. is provided in smart cities which helps in making future country plans (5).
