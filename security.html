Internet of Things

Data Sharing, Security & Privacy

The reason behind sharing data

Sharing data allows for IOT to bring more efficiency to our day-to-day lifestyle (2). It is a way for many sectors of our global economy to consider different way of providing good and services to our consumers (2). The idea behind sharing data amongst the connected devices is to provide efficiency to the users as well as our economy through the collection of this data (2).

Privacy & Security Issues

Putting data especially personal information onto the internet already poses a threat to be hacked at any time. This is something that a user is supposed to be aware of initial to posting. In the case of IOT, since all the devices are connected, there is a possibility for this data to be hacked, surveillances, monitored, location tracking etc. (2). For example, a user of a cellphone such as an iPhone usually has their smartwatch, laptop and other devices connected. However, if one of the devices was ever to be hacked, they would have access to all the data on all devices in edition to being tracked and monitored.
